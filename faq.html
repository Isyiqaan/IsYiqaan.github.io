FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions about Ultimate Personality Test.

Is the quiz free?

Yes. The Ultimate Personality Test is completely free to play.

How many questions are there?

The quiz contains 10 questions. Each answer contributes to generating your personality result.

Can I take the quiz again?

Yes. You can return after the cooldown period to receive another set of personality results and continue your streak.

Are the results scientifically accurate?

No. The quiz is made for entertainment and should not be considered a professional personality assessment.

Why do I have a streak?

Your streak rewards you for returning regularly. Taking the quiz on different days increases your streak and keeps you active on the leaderboard.

Why do I see different percentages each time?

Every completed quiz generates a fresh personality profile, making each experience unique and fun to share with friends.

How do I share my results?

After completing the quiz, use the WhatsApp sharing button on the Results page to share your personality with others.

Who can I contact if something isn't working?

Visit the Contact page and send us an email. We'll do our best to help.
